How Modiin Municipality Changed the Surveying Department

From manual validation processes to completely automated GIS workflows

ðŸ›ï¸

Modiin-Maccabim-Reut Municipality

Leading smart city in the center of the country

The Challenge

The surveying department at Modiin Municipality was overloaded with manual validation of MAVAT files, many hours were dedicated to checking format compliance. Survey data remained in fragmented CAD files, which made it difficult to maintain a comprehensive GIS database. The department struggled with integrating cadastre and ownership data, which led to delays in planning processes.

The Solution

GeomAI implemented a completely automated workflow in three stages: (1) Automatic MAVAT validation without human intervention, catching errors immediately and providing detailed fix recommendations. (2) Smart conversion of survey files to continuous GIS database, automatic extraction of block attributes and assigning them to correct polyline and polygon entities. (3) Automatic enrichment with cadastre, ownership and zoning layers, creating a comprehensive multi-layered GIS system.

The Change

The surveying department underwent a complete digital change. Manual validation processes were completely eliminated. What previously took weeks of manual work, now completes in hours, completely automatic. The department now manages a comprehensive GIS database that is continuously updated and enriched with all required layers. Planning decisions are made faster and with more confidence, based on accurate and integrated spatial data.
